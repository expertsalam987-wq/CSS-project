Citation of
Professor Abiodun Musa Aibinu

Abiodun Musa AIBINU (PhD), is an "Acado career-driven achiever with over Twenty-four (24) years of working experience in the field of mechatronics Engineering, Autotronics,
Teching and Research.
He is the Vice-Chancellor, Chief Executive and Academic officer at Summit University, Offa, Kwara state, Nigeria and a Professor of Mechatronics Engineering at the Federal University of Technology (FUT), Minna, Nigeria.

    Formerly, he was the:
  1. Head, Department of Mechatronics Engineering, FUT Minna, Nigeria: 2014 to 2022.
  2. Director, Centre of Open Distance and e-learning, FUT Minna: 2014 to 2022.
  3. Coordinator, Advanced Engineering Innovation Research Group, FUT Minna: 2013 to 2015.
  4. Chairman, Unmanned Aerial Vehicle Challenge, 2020.
Known as an advocate of Spiritual Intelligence, Artificail Intelligence, and AcadoPreneurship,

AcadoPreneur Abinu is also a co-founder at Artificial Intelligence for Clean Energy Nigeria Limited, a startup firm with product development and industry 4.0 technologies.
Professor Aibinu has participated in and won several academic and research awards at various
international and national exhibitions: he was nominated for the 2012 Promising Researcher Award and Best Teacher Award at IIUM Malaysia;
In 2017, Acado Preneur Aibinu was one of the recipients of the highly prestigious Nigerian Air Force (NAF) Research and Development Award.
Also, in 2019, he was honoured with the SERVICOM Excellence Award by the Federal University of Technology Minna, Nigeria in recognition of his selless services and contributions to the development of the Federal University of Technology Minna, Nigeria and the country at large;
In 2021, he was one of the recipients of the Vice-Chancellor Excellence Award.
